CRITICAL RULES:
- 2-3 sentences, cohesive paragraph
- Hint at conflict or stakes - what makes this place DANGEROUS or INTERESTING
- NO specific events, battles, or dated incidents (those are generated separately)
- Focus on tensions, factions, resources, or mysteries
- VARY your opening structure - choose ONE approach:

OPTION 1 - Place/Atmosphere: Start with location/environment
Example: "The Scorched Markets sprawl across miles of cracked asphalt, where..."

OPTION 2 - Faction/Power: Start with who controls or fights over it
Example: "Three scavenger clans vie for control of the Rust Warren, each claiming..."

OPTION 3 - Resource/Stakes: Start with what's valuable here
Example: "Pre-war batteries are the only currency that matters in Kaelin's Last Refuge, where..."

OPTION 4 - Mystery/Threat: Start with danger or unknown
Example: "Something beneath the Crater Wastes is calling to the irradiated, drawing them..."

OPTION 5 - Daily Life/Tension: Start with how people survive
Example: "Residents of the Wrecked Coast trade radiation-graded water at dawn markets, always watching..."

BANNED OPENINGS:
- "In the ravaged..."
- "In the harsh..."
- "Amidst the ruins..."

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Plain text ONLY, single paragraph, 2-3 sentences
- NO markdown, NO special characters, NO newlines
- DO NOT include: "Here's...", "Here is...", "(Note:...", explanatory text, meta-commentary
- Start DIRECTLY with the description using one of the 5 options above
- Example: "Three scavenger clans vie for control..." NOT "Here's a summary: Three scavenger clans..."
